Google Scholar

Cross disciplinary academic articles, theses and online book searching with article results ranked by author's importance, number of linked references and journal ranking. Advanced search features that allow access to Stanford fulltext and useful filters including for articles in Medicine, Pharmacology, and Veterinary Science. Includes patents and legal documents.

Web of Science

Citation database with multidisciplinary coverage of high-impact journals in the sciences, social sciences, and arts and humanities, as well as international proceedings. Citation linking and filtering by subject and document types.

PubMed

A public domain database of article citations across these Biomedical - Life Science - journals and periodicals. A PubMed search result on one article includes a citation: title, abstract, authors, publication, issue, volume, page plus indexing (done by subject matter experts) for publication type, age group and Medical Subject Headings (MeSH) terms.
