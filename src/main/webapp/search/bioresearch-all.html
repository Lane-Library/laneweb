Proteome is a proprietary database of highly curated proteomic data based on the literature. You will need to login and re-run the search within Proteome to see actual results.
See FAQ for obtaining a free account from Proteome (SUNet ID required). Derwent Innovations Index is a search engine for worldwide patents. GoPubMed is a search engine for querying literature in PubMed and visualizing the results according to terms from the GO ontologies. HighWire is a comprehensive full-text science-specific search engine that searches about half of the scientific literature. BIOSIS is a literature database especially helpful for finding document types not indexed in PubMed, including published abstracts of papers presented at meetings, United States patents, books and book chapters. IEEE Xplore is the foremost literature database for electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics. BioWareDB Specialized search engine with good coverage of the universe of bioinformatics softwares and databases. AbMiner is a curated database of commercially available, highly characterized antibodies for research purposes. iHOP is the fastest way to rapidly understand gene function based on statements made in the literature. DrugBank is a curated database of highly summarized yet detailed drug data (e.g., chemical, pharmacological and pharmaceutical properties), combined with comprehensive drug target data. F1000 provides reviews and ranks papers published in the sciences based on the recommendations of a faculty of over 1000 selected leading researchers. Community Academic Profiles is a directory of Stanford School of Medicine researchers, clinicians and affiliates. Community Of Science Expertise is a wide-reaching scientific expertise database focused on Stanford researchers. Community Of Science Funding Database is a comprehensive, well-structured source of funding information, complete with profile-based notificatin. Searches last 5 years of MEDLINE, WCI, Web of Science, Zoological Record, BIOSIS Previews and Derwent Innovations Index. UBC'sBioinformatics Links Directory (NAR Web server compilation) is a curated collection of bioinformatics tools. SCI is a proprietary database that provides citation frequency. DTIC is the US military's principal database for its unclassified/declassified research. Engineering Village is the foremost literature database for all aspects of the engineering literature. Science Accelerator is the US Department of Energy's database for its unclassified/declassified research. This search engine returns hits from laboratories in Stanford's Department of Engineering. The University of Pittsburgh's OBRCOnline Bioinformatics Resources Collection (OBRC) is a curated collection of bioinformatics tools.
